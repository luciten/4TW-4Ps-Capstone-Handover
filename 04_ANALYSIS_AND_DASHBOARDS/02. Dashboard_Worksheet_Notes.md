MAIN QUESTIONS ANSWERED:
* Is the 4Ps achieving uniform success across all regions of the Philippines, or are there significant performance gaps?
* Which specific regions are the highest and lowest performers in key areas of 4Ps?
* What local factors affect these performance gaps?
* Which regions do we focus on first, and what tailor-fit interventions address the specific challenges in each priority region?

HOW TO READ THE DASHBOARD/WORKSHEET:
   1. Open Dashboard/Worksheet_Overview.xlsx to see the full summary.
   2. For deeper analysis, open the Excel file for that focus area (links embedded).

CAVEATS:
- All dashboards are static (Excel-only, no interactivity).
- Different datasets came from different years.
- Some 4Ps Quarter reports are missing/incomplete.
- Some regions had missing/incomplete reports.
